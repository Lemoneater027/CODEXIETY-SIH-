About Monasteries in Sikkim

Sikkim is home to over 200 monasteries, representing the rich spiritual and cultural traditions of Tibetan Buddhism. Each monastery reflects unique architectural styles, centuries-old rituals, and vibrant festivals. From the famous Rumtek Monastery to the serene Phensang Monastery, these sacred places are not only centers of worship but also repositories of art, manuscripts, and living traditions.
