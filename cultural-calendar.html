Cultural Calendar of Sikkim

Explore the sacred festivals and monastery events happening throughout the year.

29 Feb

Losar – Tibetan New Year

Location: Rumtek & Enchey Monasteries

Description: Traditional Tibetan New Year celebrations with mask dances and prayers.

Booking: Required

15 Jun

Saga Dawa

Location: Rumtek Monastery

Description: Celebrates Buddha’s birth, enlightenment, and death.

Booking:Required

28 July

Drukpa Tsheshi

Location: Phensang Monastery

Description: Celebrate Buddha's first sermon on the Four Noble Truths

Booking: Not Required

14 Mar

Bumchu Festival

Location: Tashiding Monastery

Description: It is a spiritual event that connects nature, humanity, and spirituality, fostering cultural identity and community.

Booking: Not Required

11 Nov

Lhabab Duchen

Location: Many Monasteries

Description: Celebrates the Buddha's descent from the Trayastrimsa heaven to the human world after teaching the gods and liberating his mother.

Booking: Not Required

20 Mar

Phodong Cham

Location: Phodong Monastery

Description: The Vibrant, masked Cham dance performed at Phodong Monastery

Booking: Not Required
